WELCOME TO THE EL PATRON LIFESTYLE BRAND

BE THE BOSS. This is the spirit of El Patron. Born out of the desire to bring out the best in a man’s personal style. A salon exclusive men’s grooming brand of innovative hair and skin care products designed to elevate masculine lifestyle. Within these products you’ll find cutting-edge formulas that deliver superior results. In artisian fashion, our products are tested to meet rigorous standards set by award-winning stylists and barbers before they leave our doors. El Patron men’s grooming products are inspired by the creative spirit of the salon professional, and the man who owns his style.
